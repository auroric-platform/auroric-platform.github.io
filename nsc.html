THE ADMINISTRATION

Nation Security Council

The National Security Council's role is to advise the President on any subject he require relating to the duties of each member’s respective office.

The current Administration's National Security Council includes President Gabriel Mataleo, and the heads of all 6 of executive departments — the Secretaries of Foreign and Culture Affairs, Justice, Commerce, Safety and Defense, Energy and Resources, and Finance.

The Administration's National Security Council represents President Mataleo's vision to establish diversity in the administrative process.
