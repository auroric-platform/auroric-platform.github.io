General Election Process Starts as Population Census Begins

6/30/2021

The general election process has begun under the provisions of Executive Order 3. The President has designated the date of election to be on 1st August, which elections of the Collective Council, and Regional Councils will be held on that day.

Before the ballot takes place, a nation-wide census will be conducted from 5th to 15th July. The aim is to obtain up-to-date benchmark information on the demographic and socio-economic characteristics of the population as well as its detailed geographical distribution. The information is used for keeping an updated list of citizens and electors.

Current citizens will have to fill in a form to maintain its current citizenship and their rights to vote or stand in an election. Current citizens who have not completed the form during the census period will not be able to vote in the coming election.

End

Modernizing Cenario Station for the future of an Unified Transport Hub

6/15/2021

Sustainability is always one of the major focuses of the General Transport Authority, where the GTA has put extensive efforts into building a mixed-mode transportation network that meets the growth of the Republic in a responsible way.

To further improve access for commuters into downtown Cenario, the GTA is in the process to revitalise Cenario Station, the station that holds the title of the Republic’s gateway.

The massive station upgrade project will be delivered in 3 phases, which in part are a greater scheme ordered by the Government of Aurora’s policy to enhance transportation infrastructure and also overall access to the bloodline of the nation.

Currently the project team has completely rebuilt the original Terminal 1 building’s interior, drastically improving station illumination, wayfinding and also passenger flow.
In this current phase of the project, train services from towards Cenario Station will only be provided by the Central and Suishoku Line, where other train services will be resumed as more phases of the entire project are being delivered.

End
